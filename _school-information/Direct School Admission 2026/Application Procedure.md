<p><strong>Instructions</strong> 
<br>Application for DSA-Sec can be submitted through the online DSA-Sec Portal.
The application is free-of-charge and will be open from 6 May 2026, 11am
to 2 Jun 2026, 4.30pm. For more details, please refer to, please refer
to&nbsp;<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/secondary/dsa" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE website</a>.</p>
<p><strong>DSA-Sec Candidates Not Studying in MOE Mainstream Schools</strong>
<br>1. Candidates are to obtain a Registration Number (RGxxxxxxx) from MOE
Customer Service Centre upon the start of the DSA Exercise. Registration
number must be submitted before applications can be processed. For more
details, please refer to&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/nmsdsa-sec-application" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE website</a>.</p>
<p>2. Candidates who are International Students (IS) may submit their DSA-Sec
applications for consideration. They are to take part in the Admissions
Exercise for International Students (AEIS).</p>
<p><strong>Considerations</strong>
<br>1. Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools wisely based
on the student’s aptitudes and strengths, bearing in mind the school's
academic and non-academic requirements, and the programmes available to
develop the area of talent.</p>
<p>2. Distance from home.</p>
<p><strong>Outcome of Application</strong>
<br>1. All DSA applicants will be notified by email of the outcome: (i) Confirmed
(CO) or (ii) Waiting List (WL) or (iii) Rejected.</p>
<p>2. Students who receive at least one DSA-Sec offer (ie. Confirmed or Waiting
List) may indicate up to three school preferences. Students who are successfully
admitted to the school via DSA are expected to honour their commitment
to the school, and participate in the activities related to the talent
they are selected for from year 1-4.</p>
<p>3. DSA-Sec offers not taken up will be considered lapsed.</p>
