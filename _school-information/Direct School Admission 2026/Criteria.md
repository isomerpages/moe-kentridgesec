<p>1. Applicant must be eligible for Secondary 1 in 2027.</p>
<p>2. Applicant must be able to demonstrate interest and talent and/or have
a personal portfolio of special awards and achievements, leadership roles
in</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>DSA – Sports and Games (Badminton)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>DSA - Sports and Games (Basketball)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>DSA – Sports and Games (Floorball)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>3. Applicants who do not have prior experience may also apply. The school
will assess applicants based on the selection criteria, which may include
assessing the potential of the applicants.</p>
<p><strong>Note:</strong>&nbsp;General academic ability tests such as General
Ability (GAT) and Higher Ability Selection Test (HAST) are&nbsp;<strong>not</strong>&nbsp;required
for the selection process.</p>
