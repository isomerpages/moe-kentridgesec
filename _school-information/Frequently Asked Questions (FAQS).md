<h5><strong>A. School Information</strong></h5>
<p><strong>1. Where is the school located?</strong>
<br>We are located at 147 West Coast Road, Singapore 127368.</p>
<p><strong>2. How can I contact the school?</strong>
<br>We can be reached at +65 67731127 during the school’s operating hours.
You can also email us at krss@moe.edu.sg</p>
<p><strong>3. How can I travel to the school by public transport?</strong>
<br>The nearest MRT station is Clementi. You can also take bus services 30,
51, 143, 175, 201 and drop off along West Coast Road. Bus service 282 stops
along Clementi West Street 2. It is a 5-minute walk to the school from
this bus stop. Click&nbsp;<a href="https://kentridgesec.moe.edu.sg/school-information/contact-information/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>&nbsp;for
more information.</p>
<p><strong>4. What are the school’s operating hours?</strong>
<br>We operate from 7.00 am to 6.30 pm on school days and from 8.00 am to
5.30 pm during the school holidays. We do not operate on public holidays.</p>
<p><strong>5. What are the school terms?</strong>
<br>There are four terms in an academic year. Click&nbsp;<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/news/press-releases/20230807-school-terms-and-holidays-for-2024" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>&nbsp;to
check MOE’s School Terms and Holidays.</p>
<h5><strong>B. School Programmes</strong></h5>
<p><strong>6. What are Kent Ridge Secondary School’s distinctive programmes?</strong>
<br>All Ministry of Education (MOE) schools have two distinctive programmes:
the Applied Learning Programme (ALP) and Learning for Life Programme (LLP).
These distinctive programmes provide opportunities for students to discover
their strengths and interests, and to find greater relevance and motivation
in their learning.</p>
<p>Please click&nbsp;<a href="/programmes/distinctive-programmes/applied-learning-programme/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>&nbsp;for
more information on our ALP.</p>
<p>Our LLP focuses on Sports and Outdoor Education (Education Values of Olympism
@ Kent Ridge (EVOKE!)). Please click&nbsp;<a href="/programmes/distinctive-programmes/learning-for-life-programme/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>&nbsp;for
more information on our LLP.</p>
