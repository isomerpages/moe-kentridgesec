<h5>Class Time-tables (Semester 2, 2026)</h5>
<p>Updated 30 June 2026</p>
<p>Dear Parent(s) / Guardian,</p>
<p>The Semester 2 timetables can be found in the links below.</p>
<p>To support our students’ well-being and help them maintain their energy
and focus, the school schedules a snack break from 12.15 pm to 12.25 pm
as part of our school routine. Parents/Guardians are encouraged to pack
simple, small and portable snacks, preferably healthier options, that can
be eaten quickly and neatly to help sustain your child’s/ward’s energy
levels and support their learning.</p>
